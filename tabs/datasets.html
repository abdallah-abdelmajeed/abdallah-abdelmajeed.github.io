Datasets & Data Collection

As part of my research, I have collected and contributed to several datasets related to environmental monitoring, remote sensing, and peatland ecosystems. These datasets provide valuable resources for understanding climate change impacts and developing monitoring methodologies.

Rzecin Peatland SIF Dataset

Comprehensive dataset of sun-induced fluorescence measurements collected using UAV-mounted sensors over the Rzecin peatland ecosystem in Poland. Includes temporal variations and spatial distribution patterns.

2022-2024 SIF Peatlands

Climate Manipulation Experiment Data

Data from controlled experiments examining the effects of simulated drought and heatwave conditions on peatland vegetation. Includes photosynthesis measurements, vegetation indices, and environmental parameters.

2021-2023 Climate Change Experimental

UAV Multispectral Imagery Collection

High-resolution multispectral imagery collected using UAV platforms over various environmental study sites. Includes vegetation indices, thermal mapping, and temporal change analysis.

2019-2024 Multispectral UAV

Agricultural Monitoring Database

Collection of agricultural field data and remote sensing imagery used for precision agriculture applications. Includes crop health indicators, weed mapping, and yield prediction models.

2020 Agriculture Precision Farming
