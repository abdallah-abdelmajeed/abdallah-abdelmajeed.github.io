Interests & Activities

Beyond my academic and research pursuits, I maintain a diverse range of interests that complement my professional work and enrich my personal life.

Professional Interests

  • Drone Technology Advancement

    Following the latest developments in UAV technology, sensors, and applications for environmental monitoring.

  • Climate Change Research

    Exploring innovative approaches to study and mitigate climate change impacts on ecosystems.

  • Open Science & Data Sharing

    Advocating for open access research and collaborative data sharing platforms in environmental science.

  • Interdisciplinary Research

    Bridging gaps between environmental engineering, remote sensing, and agricultural sciences.

Personal Interests

  • Outdoor Photography

    Capturing landscapes and natural environments, particularly wetlands and forest ecosystems.

  • Hiking & Nature Exploration

    Exploring natural reserves and protected areas to observe ecosystems firsthand.

  • Science Communication

    Participating in outreach activities to communicate environmental science to broader audiences.

  • Cultural Exchange

    Learning about different cultures and languages through travel and international collaboration.

Community Involvement

I actively participate in environmental awareness campaigns and volunteer for local conservation efforts. I believe in the importance of connecting academic research with community action to address environmental challenges. Through workshops and educational programs, I aim to share knowledge about sustainable practices and the importance of ecosystem preservation.
